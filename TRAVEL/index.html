Travel is the greatest source of true knowledge.

In 2019 and before, I have been to many places, such as Portugal, Canada, Japan, Beimi, etc. And my next destination is Belize. I will participate in an MSU study program and follow the social science team to Belize for service-learning programs.

As a Spartan, participating in an education abroad program is a significant part of college life. I went a travel website and saw pictures and description of the Great Blue Hole, a giant marine sinkhole off the coast of Belize. I was deeply attracted to its beauty and want to see this masterpiece of nature with my own eyes.

For me now, Belize is a country that exists only in words and pictures. I admit that pictures and articles on the internet help us understand a country we have never been to, but these cannot replace first-hand experiences. Unlike other programs that keep students primarily in the classroom, the service-learning experience in Belize is special because it involves learning through volunteer activities.
